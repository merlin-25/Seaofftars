HEROES

Valer

❮ ❯

Valer The moon girl

Valer is the all powerful moon girl. She can use the power of the moon to her advantage and use it to destroy her enemy. She's equipped with a lot of great powers that players can use in the game.

As a moon girl, she can control the tides of the water to destroy the enemy. She can also use the moon to blinds her opponents. Despite that, she still needs someone else to destroy the Fleshmancer.

Zael

❮ ❯

Zael The sun boy

Zael is the all powerful sun boy. He can use the power of the sun to his advantage and use it to destroy his enemy. He's equipped with a lot of great powers that players can use in the game.

As a blade dancer, he can also attack his enemy with fire blades. Other things he can do is generate fire balls. Even though he is powerful, he still needs someone else to destroy the Fleshmancer.

Galr

❮ ❯

Galr The humble fisherman

Garl was born a normal human like the rest of us. He spent his day fishing and providing for his family. But behind his seemingly normal appearance, lies a great power in him.

The Gods blessed Garl with a power unlike others. This power is in the form of his defensive and healing ability. With this power, Galr joins Valer and Zael on the journey to defeat the Fleshmancer.

ENEMIES

Pawn

❮ ❯

Pawn Basic Enemy

Pawn might not look like a strong enemy. Afterall, it can only cause minimal damage to our character. However, the true strength of pawn lies in numbers. Pawns will swarm and overwhelm our character.

This enemy should not be underestimated!

Bishop

❮ ❯

Bishop Fleshmancer's right hand

Bishops are Fleshmancer's right hand. If bishops are around your character, that means you are getting closer to Fleshmancer.

Be warn, Bishops are agile and fast. They can also attack your character from any direction!

Fleshmancer

❮ ❯

Fleshmancer The Villain

Fleshmancer is the most evil among evil. The worst of the worst. He wants to disrupt the world with his huge amount of power. Fortunately, he is not unbeatable. With the combine power of the sun and moon, your character can perform eclipse magic to destory the villain at last.

Fleshmancer is the most powerful being in this world. So, good luck to all the players.
